The Problem

Managing parents' health from 500 km away is broken.

Young professionals are sole earners juggling EMIs, careers, and the constant anxiety of ageing parents in another city with no support system.

🏥
Zero health visibility
Prescriptions on paper, reports on WhatsApp, vitals nowhere. You find out about a health issue only when it's already serious.
🚨
Emergencies with no plan
Finding a reliable ambulance or nurse at midnight from 500 km away is a nightmare. Every second counts and you have no system.
💭
Constant guilt
The guilt of not being there weighs on you every day. No product today solves the emotional burden — only tasks.
How It Works

From signup to full coverage in 48 hours.

01
Onboard your parent
We send a care kit — smartwatch + SOS pendant — directly to your parent's home. Setup takes 15 minutes.
02
Digitise their health
Upload old prescriptions and reports. Our AI organises them into a clean health timeline you can access anytime.
03
Get weekly summaries
Every Sunday, receive an AI-generated health update on WhatsApp. Vitals, medications, upcoming appointments — all in 60 seconds.
04
One tap in an emergency
Your parent presses the SOS button. You're alerted instantly. Our local network handles everything on the ground.
Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for one person managing everything alone. Powered by AI so you don't need a team.

📋
Health record hub
All prescriptions, reports, and vitals digitised and organised in one place. No more WhatsApp hunting.
💊
Medication reminders
Automated reminders sent to your parent's phone. Missed doses alert you immediately.
📊
Weekly AI health brief
Plain-language summary of your parent's health every week on WhatsApp. No medical jargon.
🆘
SOS emergency response
One-press pendant alerts you and our local responder network simultaneously. Pre-filled hospital intake ready.

Nurses, doctors, diagnostics on call in your parent's city. We vet every provider so you don't have to.

Wearable health tracking
Heart rate, SpO2, steps, and fall detection — all synced automatically from the parent's smartwatch.
